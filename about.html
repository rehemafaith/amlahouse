About Us


Amla Fashion House was established in 2010 by owner Rehema Faith.
Rehema was born and raised in Mombasa and moved to the Nairobi when she was 20 years old.
Having been in retail for over 10 years,Rehema knows that it is important
to have a “hands-on” business and be involved in every aspect of the operation.
She has created a beautiful boutique with her own personal flair and decorating style.
She goes to market and does all the buying for the boutique.
Rehema stays on top of all the latest trends and makes sure to get what her clients want.
